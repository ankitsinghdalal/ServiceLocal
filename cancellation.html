Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: February 2026

At FixKaro, we understand that plans can change. This policy outlines our cancellation and refund terms to ensure a fair experience for both customers and service professionals.

1. Cancellation by Customer

You may cancel a service booking at any time before the professional arrives at your location. The refund amount depends on when you cancel:

Cancellation Timing Refund
More than 2 hours before scheduled time Full refund (if prepaid)
Less than 2 hours before scheduled time 50% refund (if prepaid)
After professional has arrived No refund; visiting charges may apply

2. Cancellation by FixKaro

In rare cases, FixKaro may cancel a booking due to:

  • No available professional in your area at the requested time.
  • Unsafe working conditions reported by the professional.
  • Incorrect or incomplete booking information.

If FixKaro cancels a booking, you will receive a full refund of any prepaid amount.

3. Rescheduling

Instead of cancelling, you may reschedule your booking at no additional cost, subject to professional availability. To reschedule, contact us at least 1 hour before your scheduled service time.

4. Refund Process

  • Refunds for prepaid bookings will be processed within 5-7 business days.
  • Refunds will be credited to the original payment method used during booking.
  • For cash payments, no refund is applicable as payment is collected after service completion.

5. Service Warranty Claims

If you are unsatisfied with the quality of work, you do not need to cancel. Instead, raise a complaint within the 30-day warranty period:

  • Contact us at support@fixkaro.in or call +91 9999-XXXXXX.
  • Describe the issue with the completed work.
  • We will send a professional to fix the issue at no additional cost.
  • If the issue cannot be resolved, a partial or full refund may be issued at our discretion.

6. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be provided in the following cases:

  • The service was completed as described and accepted by the customer.
  • The customer was not present at the scheduled time and location.
  • Cancellation after the service has been completed.
  • Damage caused by the customer's actions or pre-existing conditions not disclosed at the time of booking.

7. How to Cancel a Booking

To cancel a booking, you can:

  • Call us at +91 9999-XXXXXX
  • Email us at support@fixkaro.in

Please have your booking reference number ready when contacting us.
